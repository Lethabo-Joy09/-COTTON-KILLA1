Our History

Founded in 2024 in Johannesburg, Cotton Killa began as a pop-up selling limited-run graphic tees to local creatives. By 2025, the brand expanded to hoodies, joggers, and accessories while collaborating with artists from Braamfontein to Maboneng.

Mission

To empower self-expression through bold, comfortable streetwear rooted in African urban culture.

Vision

To be South Africa’s most loved streetwear label with a global footprint and community impact.

Target Audience

Fashion-forward 16–35 year olds who value creativity, authenticity, and community.
